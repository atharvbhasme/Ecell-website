Testimonials

Mr. Dadu Salgar

Founder at Salgar Amrutatulya Chaha

The event focussed on the entrepreneurial journey of the spokesperson. How he started many ventures from scratch but failed before starting the teaselling business Salgar Amruttulya Chaha? How he aimed to provide better products and services? And created a brand that will lead to an increase in the market value and demand of the product? The spokesperson also talked about how he runs his business and how the franchise model operates. As Salgar Chaha is a fast-growing business, he mentioned the factors affecting rapid growth.

Mr. Pankaj Dubey

MD/CEO Power Global India, Founder & CEO DSPIN Consulting

The spokesperson went through his life journey and struggled in building his position. He advised the students that they should focus on developing themselves and exploring fields like finance and investments. The start-ups must thrive to develop great products with a futuristic approach. And try to add value to them. Even though you face failure, rebuild it, improve your service quality, and gain what you lost. While sharing his life journey, he said that no is never final so keep trying. Also, give a date to your dream so that they become achievable.

Mr. Ajinkya Kulkarni

Co-founder at Talent Battle

The spokesperson started the session by speaking about how his decision led him to become an entrepreneur. He saw a problem faced by his friends which he was able to resolve. With taking his story as the reference he explained how a startup can grow and expand. He advised all the participants to be socially active and try to connect and interact on Linkedin, which will increase their knowledge and confidence.
